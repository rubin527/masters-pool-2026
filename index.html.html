⛳ The Draft

1.
Before the tournament begins, all owners participate in a snake draft to select their team of golfers from the full Masters field.
2.
Each owner selects 6 golfers. Once a golfer is selected, no other team may claim them.
3.
The draft order reverses each round — this is the snake. The owner who picks first in Round 1 picks last in Round 2, and so on.
4.
Each owner has 90 seconds to make their selection. If time expires, the system automatically selects the highest-ranked available golfer by odds.

How the snake draft works

Gold squares mark the turn ends where the order reverses.

📊 Scoring

1.
Your team score is the combined score-to-par of your top 4 performing golfers after all four rounds. Lowest score wins.
2.
Only your best 4 golfers count. The remaining 2 sit on the bench and do not affect your score — unless a cut penalty forces them into your top 4.
3.
Missed cut: A golfer who misses the cut is assigned the same score as the last-place finisher among those who made the cut. This score updates live throughout the weekend as Saturday and Sunday scores are posted. Missing the cut is meant to hurt.
4.
A golfer who withdraws (WD) after making the cut receives the same last-place penalty.
